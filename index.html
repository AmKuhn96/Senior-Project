Welcome to KoffeeGeeks in Circleville!

Owners, Mike & Jodi, are from Laurelville, Ohio, and wanted nothing more than to create the best small-batch roasted coffee possible for their communities.

"Our dream was to offer excellent coffee to our customers, without it costing half your paycheck to get a good “cuppa joe”."

"We have a beautiful 3 acres and a place for our huge roaster, affectionately called “Rosie”, so we can bring an excellent quality product to you, our customer."
